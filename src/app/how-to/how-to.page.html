How to play

The Goal of the game is to defeat all the enemies and exit the maze with the highest score posible

You can navigate through the rooms with the North, South, East, West buttons

Be careful, there are trolls blocking certain passages, but you will not know which ones. You must defeat them before you can continue that path.

Defeat the trolls with arrows that can be found in some rooms, you can obntain them with the 'Collect Arrow' button. To use them, click the 'Attack' button.

To increase your score, there are coins in each room to collect. Click the 'Collect Coin' button to obtain the coin

Once all Trolls have been defeated, you must find the exit and then the game will end.
